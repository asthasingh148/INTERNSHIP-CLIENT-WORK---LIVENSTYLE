Live N Style is a modern, aesthetically designed home décor website that showcases premium lifestyle products, including bedding, dining essentials, bath collections, and decorative items.

## 🌟 Overview

Live N Style focuses on blending **luxury, tradition, and modern design** to create a seamless online shopping experience. The platform highlights curated collections that transform everyday living spaces into elegant and meaningful environments.

## ✨ Features

- 🏠 Elegant UI for home décor presentation
- 🛍️ Product collections (Bedding, Dining, Bath, Decor, etc.)
- 🎨 Aesthetic and minimal design approach
- 📱 Responsive layout for all devices
- 🔎 Organized categories for easy navigation
- 📧 Newsletter subscription integration

## 🧩 Sections Included

- Home Page (Hero + Featured Collections)
- Product Categories (Bedding, Kitchen, Dining, Bath, Decor, etc.)
- Cultural & Lifestyle Content Sections
- Newsletter / Subscription Section
- Footer with Quick Links & Policies

## 🎯 Concept & Inspiration

The platform is inspired by the idea that:
> “Every home tells a story through its objects, culture, and design.”

It combines:
- Traditional Indian aesthetics  
- Modern luxury design  
- Functional lifestyle elements
